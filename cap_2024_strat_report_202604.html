0.1 Overview

This report includes court records related to Colorado’s Safe Storage of Firearms Law filed between April 1, 2021 and December 31, 2024. Data and metadata are pulled directly from REDCap API.

This report includes crosstabs for three categories of variables:

  1. Defendant demographics and characteristics
  2. Court case context
  3. Court case outcomes

The first and third categories are stratified by whether the safe storage offense was the primary or secondary offense.

All three categories are stratified by whether or not a child was involved in the case.

All three categories are also stratified by who illegally accessed the unsafely stored firearm(s).
